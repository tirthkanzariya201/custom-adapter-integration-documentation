**App prerequisites**

* minSdkVersion of **23** or higher
* compileSdkVersion of 36 or higher
* The Google Mobile Ads (GMA) SDK must be integrated in your app (next-generation GMA API)
* An ad unit must already be in place which will be used for targeting

**Note:** **Custom Adapter (GMA Next-Gen SDK)** covers **AdMob** and **Google Ad Manager** on **Android only**. Use the standard Custom Adapter (GMA SDK) for other mediations and platforms.
