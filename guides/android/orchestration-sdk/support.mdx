---
title: Support
description: >-
  Support for TapMind Orchestration SDK on Android.
---

Contact your TapMind Account Manager for Orchestration SDK support.

Include in your request:

- The **error code** and **error message** from `OrchError` (see [Error Reference](/guides/android/orchestration-sdk/error-reference))
- The **placement code** you are using
- Log output filtered by **`ORCH`**
- Android OS version and whether Google Play Services is available on the device
